The Importance of Dhikr

☪

Dhikr (remembrance of Allah) is among the most virtuous acts of worship in Islam. It brings peace to the heart, strengthens faith, and serves as a shield against worldly distractions.

♥

Peace of Heart

"Verily, in the remembrance of Allah do hearts find rest." - Quran 13:28

⛨

Spiritual Protection

Dhikr serves as a shield against Satan and protects the believer from spiritual harm.

⚖

Weight on the Scales

The Prophet Muhammad (PBUH) said that statements of dhikr are heavy on the scales of good deeds.
